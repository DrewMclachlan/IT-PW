About The Medical Study Database


The Medical Study Database was founded in 2020 by Drew X, Ryan X, and Emmet Young. From the beginning, the team wanted to help students find work that they could fit around their studies. They had all been students themselves, and so they appreciated how difficult it can be to find paid work that matches a student's ever-changing schedule.

After some brainstorming, the team looked at jobs in the Medical Study community and found that not only were the hours flexible, the pay was good too. So, the team made a few phonecalls to test the water, and quickly found a host of medical and psychology-based experimenters interested in contributing. Two months of work later, and the app was ready to launch. The rest, as they say, is medical history.
